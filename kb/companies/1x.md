# 1X Technologies (NEO)

*Last verified: 2026-07-09 (video #001 fact-check). Prices/specs move fast — re-verify before use.*

## Company
- Norwegian-American; founded in Norway as Halodi Robotics; CEO Bernt Børnich
- US factory: Hayward, CA — 58,000 sq ft, 200+ employees, ~10,000 robots/yr capacity, stated target 100,000+/yr by end of 2027 (Forbes, 2026-04-30)

## Funding
- Series A2 ~$23.5M led by OpenAI Startup Fund (Mar 2023)
- Series B $100M led by EQT Ventures + Samsung NEXT, Nistad Group, Sandwater, Skagerak Capital; Tiger Global also a backer (Jan 2024 — 1x.tech/discover)
- REPORTED (not confirmed closed): raising up to $1B at $10B+ valuation (The Information, Sept 2025) — say "reportedly raising," never "raised"
- Nvidia = collaboration partner, NOT a verified investor

## NEO product
- $20,000 outright OR $499/mo subscription; $200 fully refundable deposit; preorder only (1x.tech/order, fetched 2026-07-09)
- 6-month subscription minimum is PRESS-REPORTED (Fortune, 2026-02-26), not on the order page — always hedge
- Specs (1x.tech/neo): 5'6", 66 lbs, 55 lb carry, 1X Tendon Drive, 22 dB, 842 Wh / 4h runtime, machine-washable knit nylon suit
- **Zero consumer deliveries as of 2026-07-09.** First-year run (~10K) sold out ~5 days after Oct 28, 2025 preorder open; shipping promised "before end of 2026" (Q3–Q4)
- WSJ Joanna Stern demo (Oct/Nov 2025): ~5 min to load 3 items in a dishwasher, 100% teleoperated (per MKBHD characterization)

## Expert Mode (teleoperation)
- Official name: **Expert Mode** — 1X operator pilots via VR when AI can't complete a task; scheduled + approved per session in the app; owners can also self-pilot
- Privacy controls (verified Oct 2025–2026): session approval, no-go zones, default face blurring, audio masking, data-sharing opt-out, background-checked operators who don't know the home's identity
- Business model = training fleet: operator actions become training data
